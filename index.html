Steve Jobs

Design is not just what it looks like and feels like. Design is how it works. Innovation distinguishes between a leader and a follower. I want to put a ding in the universe. -Steve Jobs

1955 - 2011







Steven Paul (Steve) Jobs was a revered American entrepeneur who founded Apple Inc. with Stephen Gary Wozniak, NeXT Inc.,and Pixar Animation Studios. He is credited as one of the pioneers of the microcomputers era during the 1970's and revolutionized the way we interact with our smartphones. His attention to detail and expertise in marketing allowed Apple Inc. to flourish into the biggest company in the world by company market-cap.
